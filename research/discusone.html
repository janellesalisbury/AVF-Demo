Industry Research and Project Intent

From the supported features table that was provided, it looks to me as if quite a few of the native features are supported by both Android and iOS. The network, notification (alert, sounds and vibration) and geolocation are all supported across the board.

The iOS 3G and newer supports all native features, while the 3G does not support compass. OS 4.6-4.7 does not support accelerometer, camera, contacts, file, media, and storage

Those seem to be the pretty big areas. For the most part, both platforms support the majority.

Lesson’s learned… Don’t wait until the last minute!! Get working as soon as possible and don’t waste time. It’s precious. Google doesn’t have the best answers when trying to find information, so don’t put all your eggs in one basket.
