    Well, the US and Great Britain seem to be spending most of their time playing games from the listings I found online, while Japan is actually using helpful, intuitive software for work more than Angry Birds (they’re everywhere!!). Although the “work” apps are out there and being purchased, the entertainment sector of games seems to be growing more rapidly.

    North America(all) is approximately 37 %, Japan in approximately 46% and Europe(all) is approximately 29%. Japan is much more into technology and keeping up with the ever-changing market, while the other two are not far behind, but don’t feel the need to have the very latest, up to date OS. Development (with these conditions) will continue to soar in Japan and markets with higher usage, while other markets might struggle to keep up with advancement, financials and app development.

    Many providers offer unlimited data packages on their phones, and it is included in the total price of the selected phone plan.
